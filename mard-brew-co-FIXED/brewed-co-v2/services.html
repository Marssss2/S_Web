Services We Provide

01

☕ Coffee Catering

We bring our full barista setup to your corporate events, weddings, and private gatherings. Minimum 50 pax. Setup and teardown included.

02

🏢 Office Coffee Program

Weekly or bi-weekly deliveries of freshly roasted beans, curated for your team. Includes brew guides and equipment recommendations.

03

🎓 Barista Workshops

Hands-on sessions covering espresso fundamentals, latte art, and brew methods. Groups of 5–15. Weekend slots open.

04

🎪 Pop-Up & Events

Partner with us for your bazaar, market, or campus fair. We bring the drinks, ambiance, and brand energy.

Monthly deliveries of 250g premium single-origin beans with tasting notes, brew cards, and a seasonal pastry surprise.

06

🏫 Campus Partnerships

Discounted café access, thesis defense setups, and student events. We actively partner with universities and student organizations.
